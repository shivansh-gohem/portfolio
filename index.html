Khabar AI 🎙️

Aapke gaon ki awaaz, roz subah 9 baje.

A hyperlocal voice news agent for Telegram. Users send their pincode once — every morning at 9am they receive a 60-second Hindi voice note of top 5 district-level news stories. Built for the 600M rural Indians that national news apps completely ignore. Fully automated: FastAPI + Groq AI + Microsoft Neural TTS + APScheduler.
